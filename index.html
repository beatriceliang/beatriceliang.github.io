Hi, I'm Beatrice (or Bea or Bee)!

I graduated from Columbia University in May 2018 with a Master of Science in Computer Science. I was a member of the ROAM Lab, where I studied Reinforcement Learning and wrote my thesis on Robot Learning in Simulation for Grasping and Manipulation.

I received my Bachelor of Arts from Colby College, where I double majored in Computer Science and Mathematical Sciences with a Concentration in Statistics. While in college, I was a Teaching Assistant for Computational Thinking, Data Structures and Algorithms, and Data Analysis and Visualization. I also was a Computer Science Extern at the United States Patent and Trademark Office and took part in the Passionate on Parallel REU at the University of Illiois at Urbana-Champaign.
